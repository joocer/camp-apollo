Camp Apollo

Magic That Lasts. Even After Lights Out.

Camp doesn't end when the tent is packed away. It lingers in the notebook under your pillow, the rocket sticker on your mirror, the secret bravery badge in your sock drawer.

Because Camp Apollo was never just a place.

It was a promise: you are safe, you are seen, you are loved — and that doesn't vanish with the stars.

Little Moments. Big Courage.

Every day at Camp is filled with tiny acts of bravery. Tying shoelaces. Telling the truth. Saying “no.” Saying “yes.”

These aren't just activities — they're missions.

And every badge, every quiet “you did it” is proof that courage doesn't have to roar.

Sometimes it just whispers, and keeps going.
